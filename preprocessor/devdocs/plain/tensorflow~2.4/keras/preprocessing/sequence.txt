# Module: tf.keras.preprocessing.sequence

Utilities for preprocessing sequence data.

## Classes

`class TimeseriesGenerator`: Utility class for generating batches of temporal
data.

## Functions

`make_sampling_table(...)`: Generates a word rank-based probabilistic sampling
table.

`pad_sequences(...)`: Pads sequences to the same length.

`skipgrams(...)`: Generates skipgram word pairs.
