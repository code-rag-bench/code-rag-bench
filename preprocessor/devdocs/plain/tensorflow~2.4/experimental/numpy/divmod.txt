# tf.experimental.numpy.divmod

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's `divmod`.

    
    tf.experimental.numpy.divmod(
        x1, x2
    )
    
Unsupported arguments: `out1`, `out2`, `out`, `where`, `casting`, `order`,
`dtype`, `subok`, `signature`, `extobj`.

See the NumPy documentation for `numpy.divmod`.
