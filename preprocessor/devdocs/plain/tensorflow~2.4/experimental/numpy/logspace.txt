# tf.experimental.numpy.logspace

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's `logspace`.

    
    tf.experimental.numpy.logspace(
        start, stop, num=50, endpoint=True, base=10.0, dtype=None, axis=0
    )
    
See the NumPy documentation for `numpy.logspace`.
