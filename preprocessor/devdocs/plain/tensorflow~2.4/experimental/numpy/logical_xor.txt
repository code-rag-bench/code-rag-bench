# tf.experimental.numpy.logical_xor

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's `logical_xor`.

    
    tf.experimental.numpy.logical_xor(
        x1, x2
    )
    
Unsupported arguments: `out`, `where`, `casting`, `order`, `dtype`, `subok`,
`signature`, `extobj`.

See the NumPy documentation for `numpy.logical_xor`.
