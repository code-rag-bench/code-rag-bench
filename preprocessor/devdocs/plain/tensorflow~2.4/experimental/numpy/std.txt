# tf.experimental.numpy.std

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's `std`.

    
    tf.experimental.numpy.std(
        a, axis=None, keepdims=None
    )
    
Unsupported arguments: `dtype`, `out`, `ddof`.

See the NumPy documentation for `numpy.std`.
