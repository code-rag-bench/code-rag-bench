# tf.experimental.numpy.linspace

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's `linspace`.

    
    tf.experimental.numpy.linspace(
        start, stop, num=50, endpoint=True, retstep=False, dtype=float, axis=0
    )
    
See the NumPy documentation for `numpy.linspace`.
