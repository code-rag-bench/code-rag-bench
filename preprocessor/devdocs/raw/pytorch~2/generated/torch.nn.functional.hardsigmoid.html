torch.nn.functional.hardsigmoid

torch.nn.functional.hardsigmoid(input, inplace=False) [source]

Applies the element-wise function

Hardsigmoid(x)={0if x≤−3,1if x≥+3,x/6+1/2otherwise\text{Hardsigmoid}(x) = \begin{cases} 0 & \text{if~} x \le -3, \\ 1 & \text{if~} x \ge +3, \\ x / 6 + 1 / 2 & \text{otherwise} \end{cases}
